Who we are

Since our inception we have been steadfast in our commitment to enhancing the lives of the most vulnerable populations,including the poor, children, women, elderly and vulnerable across Uttar Pradesh and Maharashtra.

Our Mission

We explore our resources to promote a better quality of life for communities, notably vulnerable population and help them empowered by enriching their lives through education, health and sustainable livelihood skills and opportunities.

Our Vision

Gramjivi Foundation visualize a progressive and sustainable society. We envision our people to enjoy peace and dignity, and availing of their fundamental rights in all perspective.
